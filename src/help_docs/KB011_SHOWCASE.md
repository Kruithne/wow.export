# KB011: Made with wow.export
The "Made with wow.export" showcase on the home screen highlights a curated selection of projects that have utilized wow.export during their creation process. 

### Disclaimer
The projects featured in the showcase are not paid endorsements. Inclusion does not imply any official partnership, sponsorship, or affiliation between the project creators and the developers of wow.export. All featured projects are community submissions shared for inspirational purposes only.

### Feedback and Submissions
If you would like to provide feedback about the showcase, request removal of your project, or submit a project for consideration, please contact us via e-mail to **wow.export@kruithne.net** with the subject "Made with wow.export".
